## Short Description (Under 80 characters)

## Full Description
GainzAssist is your simple and honest companion for tracking workouts and staying consistent. Whether you are lifting weights, doing cardio, or just trying to stay active, GainzAssist helps you log your routines and progress easily.

Our MVP focuses on giving you the basic tools to keep a record of your exercises without overcomplicating your routine. Plan your workouts, log your sessions, and review your history to stay motivated.

## Features
- Create and organize your custom workout routines
- Log individual workout sessions step-by-step
- Edit and update existing workouts on the go
- View a comprehensive history of your fitness journey
- Access how-to videos for exercise guidance

## Target Audience
Fitness enthusiasts, beginners, and anyone looking for a simple, straightforward way to track their daily workouts and stay consistent.
