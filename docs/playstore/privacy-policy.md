# Privacy Policy for GainzAssist

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to GainzAssist. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our workout tracking and exercise guidance application ("GainzAssist").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account and Login Data
When you create an account or log in, we collect:
- **Email Address and User ID:** We use Firebase Authentication to securely manage your login process and identify your account.
- **Third-Party Logins:** We offer sign-in options through Google Sign-In and Facebook Login (if enabled). If you use these services, we may receive basic profile information (such as your name and email) from those providers.

### 2.2 Workout Data
As the core purpose of GainzAssist is workout tracking and exercise guidance, we collect the workout data you enter, which includes:
- Information about your workout sessions, including exercises, sets, reps, and weights.
- Progress data over time.
This data is synced and securely stored using Firebase Realtime Database to ensure your data is available across devices.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:
- Provide the core functionality of GainzAssist: tracking your workouts and providing exercise guidance.
- Authenticate your account and keep your data secure.
- Sync your data across devices using Firebase Realtime Database.
- Analyze app usage via Firebase Analytics (if enabled) to help us improve the application.

## 4. Third-Party Services
GainzAssist integrates with the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:
- **Firebase Authentication & Realtime Database:** Used for user authentication and storing your workout data.
- **Firebase Analytics (if enabled):** May be used to collect analytics about your app usage to improve our service.
- **Google Sign-In & Facebook Login:** Used to facilitate account creation and login.
- **YouTube API:** We may use the YouTube API to search and display how-to videos for exercises. By using this feature, you are also bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

## 5. Data Retention
We retain your personal information, including your account data and workout progress, for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the GainzAssist services.
